Leadership Message

Working for Humanity

"Amar Dhara Foundation was established with a singular vision: to bridge the gap between resources and those who have been left behind. We ensure absolute transparency in every drop of aid provided."

Our Trust Mission

To provide healthcare, educational infrastructure, and disaster relief to the grassroots of India through verified donor participation.

Pillars of Our Foundation

Education & Literacy

Providing essential learning kits, stationaries, and after-school tuition for children in rural clusters.

Health & Elder Care

Mobile health clinics and social companionship programs for isolated senior citizens without support systems.

Community Welfare

Emergency nutritional kits and community kitchens during disaster periods and extreme seasons.
